## NUOVO: GDPR parentEmail in chiaro in MAIN

### [ALTO — FIX PRONTO MA NON MERGIATO] parentEmail salvata in plaintext localStorage

**Problema**: `src/services/gdprService.js:334`
```js

    status: 'parental_sent',
```
